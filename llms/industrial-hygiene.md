## [ENTITY]

Western Health & Safety is a Certified Industrial Hygienist (CIH)-led consulting firm specializing in exposure assessment, workplace hazard evaluation, and occupational health risk management.

Industrial hygiene services focus on identifying, evaluating, and controlling workplace exposures including:

- Asbestos and airborne fibres  
- Chemical exposure (including VOCs and BTEX)  
- Dust and particulate  
- Noise exposure  
- Indoor air quality concerns
