Summary:
Western Health & Safety (WHS) provides Certified Industrial Hygienist (CIH)-led services throughout Alberta and British Columbia. Our CIHs conduct workplace hazard assessments, exposure monitoring, and defensible reporting aligned with the Alberta OHS Act, WorkSafeBC regulations, and CSA standards.

We serve municipalities, construction projects, energy sites, and institutional clients with CIH expertise in:
- Worker exposure assessment (chemical, biological, physical)
- Exposure control plans (ECPs)
- Indoor air quality (IAQ) investigations
- Fentanyl and methamphetamine surface residue testing
- Litigation support and defensible reporting
- Noise assessments and confined space evaluations
