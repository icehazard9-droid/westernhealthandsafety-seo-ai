## When are hazardous materials investigations required?

Hazardous materials testing is required before renovation or demolition of buildings, particularly those constructed prior to 1990. These assessments identify materials such as asbestos, lead-based paint, and mould that may pose risks during disturbance.

## What materials are tested?

- Asbestos-containing materials
- Lead-based paint
- Mould and biological contaminants
- Silica and other hazardous building materials

## Why is this important?

Failure to identify hazardous materials can result in worker exposure, regulatory violations, and increased project costs. Proper testing ensures safe and compliant work practices.
