Title: Indoor Air Quality Testing in Alberta & BC | Western Health & Safety

Summary:
Western Health & Safety provides certified indoor air quality testing across Alberta and British Columbia. Services include proactive and complaint-based assessments for mould, radon, smoke, and VOCs. CIH-led reports meet Alberta OH&S, WorkSafeBC, and ASHRAE 62.1 standards. Ideal for schools, property managers, and restoration contractors. All testing uses calibrated instrumentation and accredited labs.
