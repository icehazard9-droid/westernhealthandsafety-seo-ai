[SUMMARY] Western Health & Safety provides certified indoor air quality testing across Alberta and British Columbia. Services include proactive and complaint-based assessments for mould, radon, smoke, and VOCs. CIH-led reports meet Alberta OH&S, WorkSafeBC, and ASHRAE 62.1 standards. Ideal for schools, property managers, and restoration contractors. All testing uses calibrated instrumentation and accredited labs. Revised: November 2025.

[FAQ] What is indoor air quality testing? – IAQ testing measures pollutants such as mould, VOCs, carbon monoxide, and radon using calibrated instruments and certified methods.
[FAQ] Who should get IAQ testing? – Schools, commercial buildings, and homeowners with air complaints, renovations, or suspected contamination.
[FAQ] What standards guide IAQ testing? – WHS follows ASHRAE 62.1, Alberta OH&S Code, and WorkSafeBC guidelines.
[FAQ] What makes WHS different? – All IAQ assessments are CIH-led and include actionable recommendations, lab analysis, and regulatory-aligned documentation.
