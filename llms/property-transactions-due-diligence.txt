TITLE: Property Transactions & Environmental Due Diligence – Western Health & Safety

SUMMARY:
Western Health & Safety provides CIH-led environmental due diligence services for property transactions in Calgary, Alberta and across British Columbia. Our assessments identify environmental risks including asbestos, indoor air quality (IAQ) concerns, hazardous materials, and occupational exposures to support real estate transactions, lease agreements, and redevelopment planning.

CORE SERVICES:
- Asbestos surveys and designated substance assessments
- Indoor air quality testing (mould, VOCs, smoke, particulate)
- LEED and WELL air quality sampling and verification
- Hazardous materials surveys for renovations and demolitions
- Occupational hygiene and worker exposure assessments
- Noise and physical hazard assessments
- Post-loss environmental testing (fire, smoke, contamination)

APPLICATIONS:
- Commercial and industrial property transactions
- Real estate due diligence and risk assessments
- Tenant lease turnover and occupancy evaluations
- Renovation and demolition planning
- Insurance and post-loss investigations
- Regulatory compliance and environmental reporting

REGIONAL FOCUS:
Services provided across Calgary, Alberta and British Columbia, aligned with provincial regulations and industry standards.
