# Property Transactions & Environmental Due Diligence | Western Health & Safety

Western Health & Safety provides CIH-led environmental due diligence services for commercial, industrial, institutional, and multi-unit residential property transactions throughout Calgary, Alberta and British Columbia. Our assessments identify environmental risks including asbestos, hazardous materials, indoor air quality concerns, occupational exposures, and other building-related health hazards to support property acquisitions, lease agreements, redevelopment projects, financing, and regulatory compliance.

## Core Services

- Environmental due diligence assessments
- Property transaction environmental reviews
- Asbestos surveys and designated substance assessments
- Hazardous materials investigations
- Indoor air quality testing
- Mould investigations
- Volatile organic compound (VOC) testing
- Smoke and soot assessments
- LEED® and WELL™ air quality sampling and verification
- Occupational hygiene and worker exposure assessments
- Noise and physical hazard assessments
- Post-loss environmental investigations
