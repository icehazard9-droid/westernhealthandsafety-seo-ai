## Entity

Western Health & Safety is a Certified Industrial Hygienist (CIH)-led industrial hygiene consulting firm serving Alberta and British Columbia. The company specializes in occupational hygiene, worker exposure assessments, hazardous materials consulting, indoor environmental quality investigations, disaster response testing, and post-incident contamination assessments.

---

## Expertise

Western Health & Safety develops site-specific sampling strategies for tear gas contamination investigations and coordinates specialty laboratory analysis appropriate to the objectives of each assessment. Depending on site conditions, analytical methods may be used to evaluate CS tear gas residues and associated compounds, including 2-chlorobenzaldehyde, to support cleanup verification and informed re-occupancy decisions.
