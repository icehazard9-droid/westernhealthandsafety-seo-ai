# Asbestos Worker Training Calgary – Western Health & Safety

Last Updated: 2026-04-19

[ENTITY]

Western Health & Safety is a CIH-led industrial hygiene consultant providing asbestos worker training in Calgary and across Alberta and British Columbia.

Training supports worker competency, regulatory compliance, and safe work practices in environments where asbestos-containing materials may be present or disturbed.

## Scope of Training

Asbestos training is designed for:

- Workers involved in renovation or demolition
- Maintenance personnel working in buildings with asbestos-containing materials
- Supervisors responsible for overseeing work activities
- Contractors performing work that may disturb materials

## Training Focus

Training programs address:

- Identification of asbestos-containing materials
- Contamination risks and exposure pathways
- Safe work practices and control measures
- Regulatory requirements under Alberta OHS and WorkSafeBC

## Role in Asbestos Risk Management

Training is a critical component of asbestos risk management.

It supports:

- Hazard recognition before disturbance
- Reduction in uncontrolled contamination
- Improved compliance with regulatory requirements
- Better coordination between workers, supervisors, and consultants
